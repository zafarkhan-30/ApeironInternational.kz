our services

Comprehensive Logistics Solutions

Multimodal Freight Solutions

We integrate air, sea, road, and rail transport under one streamlined service to optimize cost, speed, and efficiency.

  • Combined transport coordination
  • Reduced transit times and handling costs
  • Centralized tracking and documentation management

Air Freight

Swift and secure air freight solutions through trusted global airline partnerships.

  • Global import and export via major international airports
  • Express, time-definite, and next-flight-out solutions
  • Airport-to-airport and door-to-door service
  • Handling of perishable, hazardous, or high-value shipments

Sea Freight

Comprehensive sea freight services via Jebel Ali Port — one of the world's busiest and most advanced ports.

  • Full Container Load (FCL) and Less-than-Container Load (LCL)
  • Cargo consolidation, cross-stuffing, and transshipment
  • Project cargo and heavy-lift solutions
  • Competitive rates for large-volume shipments

Road Transport

Flexible road freight services across the UAE and GCC countries.

  • Local and national distribution within the UAE
  • Cross-border trucking to Saudi Arabia, Oman, Kuwait, Bahrain, and Qatar
  • Full-Truckload (FTL) and Less-than-Truckload (LTL) options
  • Container drayage and bonded transport services

Rail Freight

Cost-effective rail solutions for long-distance or bulk transportation.

  • Regional and international rail routes, including UAE–CIS corridors
  • Seamless integration with sea and road transport
  • Ideal for non-urgent or large-volume shipments

Customs Clearance

Expert handling of customs formalities to ensure smooth clearance and full compliance.

  • UAE import and export declarations
  • Documentation for Free Zone and mainland operations
  • HS code classification and regulatory compliance
  • Management of duties, exemptions, temporary imports, re-exports, and ATA Carnets

Warehousing & Distribution

Stackable warehousing solutions within JAFZA, offering seamless access to key transport infrastructure.

  • Short-term and long-term storage options
  • Temperature-controlled and general warehousing
  • Inventory management and real-time tracking
  • Order fulfillment, pick and pack, and dispatch services
  • Local distribution and last-mile delivery

Project Cargo & Specialized Logistics

Customized logistics for heavy, oversized, or technically complex shipments.

  • Industrial equipment and out-of-gauge (OOG) cargo
  • Route surveys, lift planning, and permit coordination
  • End-to-end management from origin to final installation
  • On-site supervision and delivery scheduling
